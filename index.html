Doraemon's Gadgets

Let's explore some of the coolest gadgets from Doraemon!

1. Anywhere Door (どこでもドア)

A magical door that allows you to travel anywhere in the world!

2. Time Machine

This machine lets you travel to any point in the past or future!

3. Bamboo-Copter (タケコプター)

A small propeller worn on the head to fly through the air.
